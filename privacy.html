Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Overview

Nadir is a self-hosted, open-source tool. Privacy is not a feature we added. It is the architecture. Your data stays on your infrastructure by design.

2. What Nadir Does NOT Collect

  • Nadir does not phone home or send telemetry
  • Nadir does not store, log, or transmit your prompts to us
  • Nadir does not access your API keys outside of your local instance
  • Nadir does not track your usage or behavior
  • Nadir does not use cookies or analytics on your self-hosted instance

3. The getnadir.com Website

The getnadir.com website (this site) is a static HTML site. We collect no personal data, use no cookies, and have no analytics or tracking scripts. We do not use advertising networks.

4. Third-Party LLM Providers

Nadir routes your prompts to LLM providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) using your API keys. These providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies. Nadir sends only the prompt content needed for each request. No additional metadata, user information, or tracking data is appended.

5. GitHub Repository

The Nadir source code is hosted on GitHub. If you interact with the GitHub repository (starring, forking, opening issues), GitHub's privacy policy applies to those interactions.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact info@getnadir.com or open an issue on GitHub.
